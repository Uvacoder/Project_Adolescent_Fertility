Teenage mothers

The beginning of fertility in adolescence changes the lives of young women in profound ways.

Early childbearing changes their entire environment and limits their options to decide how to lead the rest of her life.

The World Health Organization (WHO) defines adolescence as the “period of life in which the individual acquires reproductive capacity, transitions from childhood to adulthood psychological patterns, and consolidates his/her socioeconomic independence”.
